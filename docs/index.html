What is a donor file?

ClearDMR uses a vocoder — the audio codec that encodes and decodes DMR voice. Due to licensing restrictions, this codec cannot be bundled with ClearDMR. Instead, the flasher extracts it from your radio's original factory firmware, which you already own a license to use.

Obtain the original firmware .bin for your specific radio model from the manufacturer's website, then select it below. The file never leaves your browser.

Connect Your Radio

Put your radio in flash mode first

  1. Turn the radio off.
  2. Hold PTT + SK1 (the two buttons on the left side) while plugging in the USB cable to your computer.
  3. The radio screen should stay blank — that confirms it is in flash mode.
