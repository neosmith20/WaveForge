Write to Radio

Writes your codeplug to the radio’s staging area via WebUSB. After writing, unplug the USB cable and power on the radio normally — ClearDMR will import the codeplug on the next boot.

  1. Hold PTT + SK1 simultaneously.
  2. While holding both, turn the radio on.
  3. The LED will flash red/green to confirm DFU mode.
  4. Click Connect & Write below and select your radio.
WebUSB requires Chrome, Edge, or another Chromium-based browser. Firefox and Safari are not supported.

What’s your callsign?

Your callsign is your radio identity — it’s stored in the radio and shown on other operators’ screens when you transmit. It’s issued by your country’s telecommunications authority (FCC in the USA, Ofcom in the UK, etc.).

Up to 8 characters • Letters and numbers only

🔌

What’s your DMR ID?

Your DMR ID is a unique 7-digit number linked to your callsign. Other operators see it when you call them, and the radio uses it to identify you on the network. Registration is free and only needs to be done once.

Don’t have one yet? Register free at radioid.net — approval takes 24–48 hours.
